Features

Everything your recipe book needs

🔗

Import from any URL

Paste a link from any recipe website. Onmenu scrapes the page and extracts ingredients and steps automatically.

📷

Photograph recipes

Point your camera at a cookbook or handwritten recipe card. OCR reads the text and AI structures it instantly.

✨

AI-powered parsing

Paste raw text from anywhere. The AI extracts title, ingredients with precise measurements, steps, tags, and nutrition.

📚

Recipe books

Organise recipes into books and share them with family or friends. Invite collaborators to add their own favourites.

🍳

Cook mode

Step-by-step cooking view with built-in timers. Swipe through each step hands-free. Scales servings automatically.

📡

Works offline

All recipes are cached locally. Cook without a signal, then sync when you're back online.

How it works

From source to table in seconds

1

Import from anywhere

Share a URL from Safari, paste text, or photograph a page. Onmenu handles any format — even recipes in other languages are translated to English automatically.

2

Review and save

The AI extract is shown for review before saving. Adjust the title, servings, or tags, then confirm. Your recipe is stored and available offline.

3

Cook with confidence

Open cook mode for a distraction-free step-by-step view. Scale the recipe for your group size, set timers, and leave notes for next time.

4

Share with your people

Create a shared recipe book, invite family or friends with a link, and build a collection together.
